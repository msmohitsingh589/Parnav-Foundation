Parmartham Navaagata

Having seen a large number of people starving to death just because of food and lack of opportunities to earn a living, a lot of children deprived of opportunities to explore and excel themselves and a number of elder persons not having basic facilities. We the team Parmartham Navagaata is always there to support and help such people to the every extent possible. At first we aim to give such people basic facilities and develop skills or prune their talent so that they can earn their own living. Parmartham Navaagata is for everyone who needs support, whether it is a poor family, deprived kids, elder persons, beggars or teens in depression. If you find someone needy please inform us.
